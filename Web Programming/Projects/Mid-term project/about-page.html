About Us

The Computer Science Club was formed to generate, advance, and share studentsâ€™ interests in Computer Science and Technology. The club meets weekly, and the club members decide on activities to be pursued in any particular academic year to help students understand up-to-date computer technology, review current hardware and software products, explore different areas of interest, and create a social environment where students with various technology experiences come together and share information with one another. The Computer Science Club is a collection of individuals that have an interest in the Computer Science field, as well as are the student representatives of the Computer and Information Science Department. This club is open to all students regardless of major, year in school, or previous experience in computing. The club's goal is to educate people in the community on Computer Science and promote it.
